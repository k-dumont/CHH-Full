Research & Development

I’ve spent 18 months developing and testing our first set of health, wellness and medicinal hemp-based products. Our initial focus has been on CBD (cannabidiol) based products, which can provide people with all kinds of health benefits without any of the intoxicating effects that accompany the other popular cannabinoid, THC. That’s not to say that THC doesn’t have its place. In fact, THC is arguably the most powerful cancer killer found in nature.
